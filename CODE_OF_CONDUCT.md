# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or public shaming
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and may remove or reject contributions that violate this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately.
